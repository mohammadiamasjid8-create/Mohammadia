The Challenge & Our Plan

Directly in the Path of Clearance

We have received official notice that Mohammadia Masjid is directly in the path of a government 24-meter road widening project. As shown by the official Pune Municipal Corporation notice and images below, the roads are already being dug up, and the marking clearly cuts into the Masjid property. To save it from complete demolition, we must act immediately to reorganise and rebuild.

Our Reconstruction Goals (~1 Crore INR Budget):

  • Secure the structure against the road widening impact.
  • Construct a proper staircase for safe access.
  • Build a dedicated Wazu (ablution) area and additional toilets.
  • Construct an additional floor for a spacious Namaz area.

The Importance of This Masjid

By saving this Masjid, you are preserving the only place of worship for an entire community and an essential facility for travellers, multiplying the reward of your Sadaqah-e-Jariyah.

This is the only masjid in Mundhwa. Its location makes it irreplaceable:

  • Only Masjid in Mundhwa: It serves the thousands of Muslim residents living in this rapidly growing area. If demolished, the community will be left with no place for daily prayers, Friday prayers, or Eid gatherings.
  • Nearest Masjid to the Railway Station: Mohammadia Masjid is the only masjid located near Hadapsar Railway Station. The station is now a fully developed stop where all express trains on major routes (including Hyderabad and Solapur) halt. This makes our Masjid a vital spiritual waypoint for countless Muslim passengers passing through Pune daily.

Join Us for Prayer

Current Situation: Our Masjid is not big enough to accommodate the local crowd due to a severe shortage of space. In fact, we are currently hosting two Jamaats for Jumma prayers just to fit everyone.

We warmly invite you to visit Mohammadia Masjid, join us for your daily prayers, and witness the pressing need of our community firsthand. Your generous donations will help us build a more spacious and permanent house of Allah.
